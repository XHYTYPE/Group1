About Us

Our Mission

Our goal is to create and deploy a user-friendly Registration and Appointment Scheduling System for the Barangay Health Center in order to improve the accessibility and efficiency of healthcare services in our community. Our objective is to simplify the patient registration and appointment management processes, ensuring that people obtain timely and effective healthcare services, and ultimately boosting the health and well-being of our community.
